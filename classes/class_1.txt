KitchenWare

attributes:
numStock (integer)
typeOfKitchenware (string)
isClean (boolean)
usedForPreppingOrEating (string)
currentMealUtensils (array)


methods:
useItem (updates isClean to false)
buyStock (increases numStock integer)
nextCourse (remove used items from currentMealUtensils array and add kitchenware needed for next meal course)
breakDish (decrease numStock integer by 1)
